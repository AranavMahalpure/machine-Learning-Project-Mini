Customer Segmentation

We have segmented the customers into 4 groups:
Group 1: Definitely a parent, max 4 family members, at least 2. Many are single parents with teenagers at home, relatively older.
Group 2: Definitely not a parent, max 2 family members, mostly couples, high income group, spans all ages.
Group 3: Majority are parents, max 3 family members, mostly one kid, typically not teenagers, relatively younger.
Group 4: Definitely a parent, max 5 family members, at least 2, many have teenagers at home, relatively older, lower income group.
